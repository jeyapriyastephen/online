About Us

Lifecare Ayurvedic Hospital was established in 2004 by Dr P R Rahul,an accomplished Ayurvedic physician with a rich and varied experience spanning over 17 years.The hospital is situated in serene surroundings of River Periyar and is enriched with a wide river frontage.Dr.Rahul is an authorised Medical Officer for Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO)since 2001. She also serves as a medical consultant to several government and private institutions.

Lifecare offers curative as well as general health therapies for a wide range of ailments. We Specialize in spine disorders and female health problems. Strict adherence to traditional Ayurvedic principles and treatment methods. Congenial ambience in which patients positively react to the treatment and healing process.Unique and integrated approach to spine care using medication and after-care along with yoga and physiotherapy.
